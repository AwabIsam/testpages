Why do we learn HTML

HTML, or HyperText Markup Language, is the cornerstone of the digital world. It's the language that structures and formats web content, making it understandable to both humans and computers. Without HTML, the internet as we know it wouldn't exist. It's the foundation upon which websites are built, enabling us to share information, communicate, and interact online. In the digital age, HTML is not just important; it's indispensable.
